Admissions

Join a Christ-centered community that cultivates excellence, integrity, and purpose.

Welcome to Our Admissions Process

Thank you for considering Sacred Harvesters International Christian School for your child’s education. We believe every child is uniquely gifted and called by God for a purpose. Our admissions process is designed to get to know your child holistically—academically, spiritually, and personally—to ensure we can help them thrive.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Passport-sized Photographs (2)
  • Pastor’s/Character Reference (optional)

Our goal is to make the admissions journey smooth and transparent. We welcome children from diverse backgrounds who are eager to learn and grow in a Christian environment. Once we receive your application, our admissions team will reach out to schedule a short assessment and parent meeting.

How to Apply

Step 1: Apply Online or In-Person

Fill out the application form on our website or visit our admissions office to apply in person.

Step 2: Assessment & Interview

Your child will take a short academic and character assessment followed by a family interview.

Step 3: Acceptance & Enrollment

Successful applicants will receive an acceptance letter and details for enrollment confirmation.

Important Dates

Event Date Details
Application Period Opens January 10, 2025 Online and in-person applications accepted.
Entrance Assessment Week March 3–7, 2025 Students sit for grade-level assessments and interviews.
Admission Offers Released March 15, 2025 Accepted students receive letters via email and mail.
New Student Orientation August 1, 2025 Mandatory orientation for all newly admitted students and parents.
